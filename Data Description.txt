Features (Columns):

Invoice – Unique invoice identifier for each transaction.

StockCode – Unique product/item code.

Description – Product description (some missing values).

Quantity – Number of items purchased in the transaction.

InvoiceDate – Date and time of purchase.

Price – Unit price of the product.

Customer ID – Unique customer identifier (some missing).

Country – Customer’s country.

Customer_Age – Customer’s age.

Gender – Customer gender (Male/Female).

Signup_Date – When the customer registered.

Last_Login_Date – Last time customer logged into the platform.

Customer_Segment – Category of customer (e.g., Regular, VIP, New).

Marketing_Channel – Source of acquisition (Organic, Referral, Paid Ads, etc.).

Category – Product category (e.g., Home Decor, Clothing).

Subcategory – More specific product classification.

Cost – Cost incurred per unit item.

Discount_Applied – Binary flag (1/0) whether a discount was applied.

Payment_Method – Mode of payment (Credit Card, PayPal, Cash, Bank Transfer).

Promo_Applied – Binary flag for promotional usage.

Delivery_Time_Days – Delivery lead time in days.

Revenue – Total revenue generated from the transaction.

Profit – Profit earned from the transaction.

Churn_Flag – (1 = churned customer, 0 = active customer).
